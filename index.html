विवाह / Marriage

In this most important ritual, Agni is lighted and by performing Parikrama inthepresenceofAgnidev, bride and bridegroom take oath and perform Sankalp of adhering to Dharma assigned to each of them by this Lagna-Sanskar. And one gets rid of Pitru-run.

ब्रतबन्ध / Bratabandha

This ritual is performed in front of Guru by accepting a three- layered cotton thread popularly known as Yagnopavit. After accepting and wearing this Yagnopavit, a person is know as Dwij – born second time. In the knot of Yagnopavit are said to be residing Brahma-Vishnu-Shiv and in nine Sutra Tantu is said to be performed poojan of Omkar, Agni, Shesh, Chandra, Pitru, Prajapati, Agni, Surya, Vishwadev.

वास्तुशास्त्र / Vastu Shastra

Vastu Shastra is an ancient science. The architecture chapter of the Yajurveda has a discourse on Vastu Shilpasastra. Vastu Shilpasastra is divided into two parts – Devashilpa and Humanshahilpa. In Devashilpa, the method of construction of religious structures such as monasteries, yajnashalas etc. is suggested, while in Manavashilp, the directions and angles to be taken into consideration while making different rooms of residential buildings are mentioned in detail. It teaches the knowledge of how to build a house. Living in a house built according to Vastu Shastra brings happiness, peace and prosperity. Staying in such a house gives a feeling of excitement, joy and happiness In Vastu Shastra information is given as to what part of the house should be.

कुण्डली / Kundali

Tithi, Bar, Month, Prahar, Karana, Yoga etc. are also important in Faladesh. Nakshatras are also given importance in Eastern astrology. Nakshatras are calculated on the basis of the period in which the moon revolves around the earth. There are 27 Nakshatras in total. A Nakshatra is divided into four paws and each paw is associated with a letter. Nine such letters are included in one rashi. The name of the native is kept from one of these letters. A total of 108 letters of the twelve zodiac signs spell the names of all people! Thus, the zodiac sign of a Jataka is determined on the basis of the moon in the eastern tradition. But in the western tradition it is determined based on the sun. In popular language, the tendency to extend the same thing by connecting it is called 'twelve twenty-seven things'. It indicates the twelve zodiac signs and twenty-seven Nakshatras. It means that no matter how much you talk about it, it doesn't end. Numerology in Astrology, Question astrology etc. are also popular. Paramahansa Swami Vishudhananda had yoga astrology. Similarly, many types of lore related to Tantramantra exist in the society. From pretsadhana, masansadhana related to tantra to dhami, jhankri all of them have the knowledge of seeing the ocean and predicting

वेद-पुराण / Ved Puran

Vedas are the greatest scriptures of Hindus. It is also considered as the father of all religious texts of the world . It is the name of the ancient sacred texts of Hinduism. It is also considered the world's first scripture. What is believed is that the Vedas are based on the knowledge imparted by God to sages. That is why it is also called Shruti. In common parlance, Veda means knowledge. The word Veda is derived from the Sanskrit word "vid" which means: to know, knowledge etc

ग्रहशान्ति / Graha Shaanti

Planets, stars and constellations in the sky contain the energies of the five elements (Earth (Matter), Water (Force), Energy, Air (Quark) and Sky (Space). The body receives the energy of the planets and constellations, the negative energy or fruit of those planets and constellations, according to the Vedic method, the method of converting the negative energy into positive energy is called planetary peace in the scriptures.
Our planet's dasha is the effect of the planet, so no matter where we are, whichever planet is affecting us the most through the five elements, that is our planet's dasha. Through the sound of Vedic or Tantric mantras, havans, text stotras, etc., the energy of the related planets and constellations works to connect with the earthly body created by our five elements, so auspicious results and positive energy can be obtained, which In addition to giving the desired, chosen, desired fruit, it also provides stability to the value.
